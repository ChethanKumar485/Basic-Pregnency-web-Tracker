📋 Birth Plan & Hospital Bag
Build your birth preferences and pack your hospital bag — print or save for your provider.
👜 For Mom
  • ID, insurance card, hospital paperwork
  • Comfortable robe / nightgown (front-opening for nursing)
  • Warm socks & non-slip slippers
  • Toiletries: toothbrush, hairbrush, lip balm
  • Maternity pads (hospital usually provides, but pack extra)
  • Dark, high-waisted disposable underwear
  • Nursing bras (2)
  • Going-home outfit (loose, comfortable)
  • Phone charger (long cable)
  • Snacks for labour & after (dates, nuts, energy bars)
👶 For Baby
  • Onesies / sleepsuits (3–4, newborn + 0-3m)
  • Swaddle blankets (2–3)
  • Baby hat & mittens
  • Newborn diapers (small pack)
  • Wipes & small towel
  • Car seat (installed & inspected!)
  • Going-home outfit for baby
  • Formula/bottle (if not exclusively breastfeeding)
🤝 For Partner / Support
  • Camera / extra phone storage
  • Change of clothes (1–2 sets)
  • Travel pillow & blanket
  • Snacks & water bottle
  • Cash/cards for parking, vending machines
  • Music playlist / earphones for labour
  • Emergency contact list printed
💡 When to pack: Have your bag ready by week 36. Keep it near the door or in the car. Pack a smaller "labour bag" for items you'll need immediately, separate from the postpartum stay bag.
